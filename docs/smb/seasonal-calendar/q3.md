# Q3 — July, August, September

## July

### Universal hooks

- **Independence Day** (Jul 4) — types: all — Holiday hours, festive posts, community events, patriotic themes. Restaurants: BBQ specials. Retail: sales. Campground: peak weekend.
- **Summer peak** — types: all — Whatever your business does in summer, this is the busiest stretch. Content should be frequent and light — photos, quick updates, event recaps.

### Industry hooks

- **Peak tourism** — types: hospitality, campground, restaurant, museum, brewery — Maximum occupancy. Focus on guest experience, real-time updates ("still have sites for this weekend"), last-minute availability.
- **Summer produce peak** — types: farm, grocery, restaurant — Tomatoes, corn, berries, stone fruit. Recipe posts, "what's at peak right now," preservation tips.
- **Summer reading** — types: bookshop — Mid-program updates, staff summer picks, reading challenge check-ins.
- **Fire safety** — types: campground, insurance, trades, government — Fireworks safety, wildfire awareness, burn ban updates, fire extinguisher reminders.
- **National Ice Cream Day** (3rd Sunday) — types: restaurant, farm, grocery — Specials, local ice cream spotlights, flavor features.
- **Summer grooming** — types: salon, pet-services — Summer hair trends, pet summer cuts, heat-related grooming tips.
- **Mid-year financial check** — types: accounting, credit-union, insurance — Review financial goals, adjust withholding, retirement contribution reminders.
- **Summer project season** — types: trades, hardware — Deck builds, painting season, outdoor projects, landscaping. Before/after features.
- **Vacation rental peak** — types: hospitality — Guest reviews rolling in. Share positive reviews (with permission), respond to all reviews, highlight amenities.
- **Summer camp mid-season** — types: childcare, youth-org, campground — Activity photos (with consent), camper spotlights, registration for late-summer sessions.

---

## August

### Universal hooks

- **Back to school** (timing varies) — types: all — Relevant for any business with families as customers. School supply drives, schedule adjustments, "last days of summer" energy.
- **Late summer / end-of-summer** — types: all — "Last chance" messaging for summer activities, products, experiences.

### Industry hooks

- **Back-to-school peak** — types: childcare, education, retail, bookshop, salon — Enrollment, school supplies, first-day haircuts, backpack drives, tutoring registration.
- **National Farmers Market Week** (1st full week) — types: farm — Market celebrations, vendor spotlights, cooking demos, customer appreciation.
- **Late summer travel** — types: hospitality, campground — Last summer trips, Labor Day weekend bookings, shoulder season rates. Lead time: 2–3 weeks.
- **World Photography Day** (Aug 19) — types: photography — Portfolio showcases, photography tips, behind-the-scenes, client features.
- **Hurricane / severe weather prep** — types: insurance, trades, government, hardware — Hurricane season peaks Aug–Oct. Preparation guides, emergency kits, insurance coverage explainers, generator and supply check.
- **Fall enrollment push** — types: fitness, education — September start dates for classes, programs, personal training. Lead time: 2–3 weeks.
- **Harvest season begins** — types: farm, brewery (winery), grocery — Early fall crops, harvest photos, u-pick announcements, winery crush season.
- **Dog days of summer** — types: pet-services, animal-shelter — Heat safety for pets, hydration tips, indoor activity ideas.
- **Summer clearance** — types: retail, hardware — End-of-season sales, inventory movement, back-to-school deals.
- **Pre-fall home prep** — types: trades, cleaning — Gutter cleaning, fall HVAC maintenance scheduling, weatherization. Book now before the rush.

---

## September

### Universal hooks

- **Labor Day** (1st Monday) — types: all — Last summer weekend, sales events, holiday hours, community events.
- **Fall begins** (Sep 22/23) — types: all — Seasonal transition content, fall hours, cozy vibes, autumn product/service features.
- **Hispanic Heritage Month** (Sep 15–Oct 15) — types: all — Community stories, cultural celebrations, bilingual content, local partnerships.

### Industry hooks

- **Fall festival season** — types: farm, brewery, community-theater, museum, campground — Apple picking, corn mazes, Oktoberfest, fall plays, harvest festivals, fall foliage trips. Lead time: 3–4 weeks for event promotion.
- **Banned Books Week** (last week) — types: bookshop — Displays, reading lists, events, freedom to read advocacy.
- **Oktoberfest** — types: brewery, restaurant — Märzen releases, German food specials, stein hoisting, themed events. Runs through October.
- **Fall mini-sessions** — types: photography — Fall foliage sessions, family portraits, holiday card photos. Lead time: 3–4 weeks for booking.
- **Estimated tax deadline** (Sep 15) — types: accounting — Payment reminders, Q4 tax planning, year-end strategy teasers.
- **Back-to-routine** — types: fitness, salon, healthcare — Post-summer recommitment. New class schedules, fall wellness checkups, seasonal style updates.
- **Fall planting** — types: farm, hardware — Cover crops, fall garden guides, bulb planting, tree planting season.
- **Pre-holiday booking** — types: florist, photography, hospitality, restaurant, salon — Holiday season fills up fast. "Book now for November/December" CTAs. Lead time: start in September.
- **Homecoming / school events** — types: florist, salon, photography — Corsages, updos, event photography.
- **Fall cleaning** — types: cleaning, laundry — Seasonal deep cleans, rug cleaning, winter wardrobe prep, closet turnover.
- **Open enrollment preview** — types: insurance, healthcare — Health insurance open enrollment starts in November. "Here's what to prepare" previews.
