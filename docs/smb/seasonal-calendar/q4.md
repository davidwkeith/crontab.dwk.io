  # Q4 — October, November, December

## October

### Universal hooks

- **Halloween** (Oct 31) — types: all — Themed content, events, costumes, decorations, trick-or-treat info. Restaurants: themed menus. Retail: seasonal products. Farm: pumpkin patches. Lead time: 2–3 weeks.
- **Breast Cancer Awareness Month** — types: healthcare, fitness, salon — Pink campaigns, screening reminders, community walks, awareness posts.

### Industry hooks

- **Adopt a Shelter Pet Month** — types: animal-shelter, pet-services — Adoption events, fee specials, adoption success stories, volunteer drives.
- **Fire Prevention Week** (2nd week) — types: trades, insurance, hardware, government — Home fire safety, smoke detector checks, escape plans, extinguisher maintenance.
- **Harvest season peak** — types: farm, grocery, brewery (winery/cidery), restaurant — Pumpkins, apples, cider, squash. Harvest festival content, seasonal menus, u-pick events, crush/fermentation updates.
- **Fall foliage** — types: hospitality, campground, photography — Peak leaf season in many areas. Foliage updates, scenic drives, photo opportunities, weekend packages.
- **Day of the Dead** (Nov 1–2, prep in Oct) — types: funeral-home, florist, house-of-worship — Altar/ofrenda flowers, memorial services, cultural education, community events.
- **Pre-holiday push** — types: retail, bookshop, florist, salon — Holiday product previews, gift guide planning, appointment booking for November/December.
- **Heating season starts** — types: trades, hardware — Furnace tune-ups, space heater safety, winterization reminders.
- **Small Business Saturday preview** — types: all — Start building awareness for the Nov event. "Support local" messaging, partnership announcements. Lead time: begin mid-October.
- **Open enrollment prep** — types: insurance, healthcare — What to know before open enrollment, plan comparison tips.
- **Cybersecurity Awareness Month** — types: accounting, credit-union, insurance, government — Online safety tips, phishing awareness, password best practices.
- **Breast Cancer Awareness walks/events** — types: fitness, nonprofit — Team registrations, training groups, fundraising recaps.

## November

### Universal hooks

- **Thanksgiving** (4th Thursday) — types: all — Gratitude posts, holiday hours, community thankfulness. Restaurants: Thanksgiving menus/catering. Grocery: holiday recipes. Farm: Thanksgiving market.
- **Veterans Day** (Nov 11) — types: all — Honor and thank veterans, community events, veteran discounts. Funeral-home, government especially relevant.
- **Black Friday / Small Business Saturday / Cyber Monday** (Fri–Mon after Thanksgiving) — types: all — Small Business Saturday is the big one for local businesses. Promotions, "shop local" messaging, open house events, special hours.

### Industry hooks

- **Year-end giving push** — types: nonprofit, food-bank, animal-shelter, social-services, house-of-worship, youth-org, museum, community-theater — Most charitable giving happens in November/December. Year-end appeals, impact stories, matching gift campaigns, tax-deductible giving reminders.
- **National Hospice Month** — types: funeral-home, healthcare — End-of-life care resources, pre-planning promotion, family support content.
- **Hunger and Homelessness Awareness Week** (week before Thanksgiving) — types: food-bank, social-services — Food drives, volunteer recruitment, impact statistics, client dignity stories.
- **Movember** — types: healthcare, salon — Men's health awareness, prostate/testicular cancer, mental health, mustache events.
- **Holiday gift guides** — types: retail, bookshop, florist, brewery, salon, farm, hardware, photography — Gift guide blog posts, curated collections, "gifts under $50" features, gift card promotions. Lead time: publish by mid-November.
- **Holiday catering** — types: restaurant, farm, grocery — Thanksgiving sides, pies, meal kits, catering menus. Pre-order deadlines. Lead time: 2–3 weeks.
- **Holiday cards / family photos** — types: photography — Last call for holiday card sessions. Lead time: shoot by mid-November for card delivery.
- **Winterization** — types: trades, hardware, campground — Pipe insulation, furnace service, campground winterization, snow equipment prep.
- **Open enrollment** — types: insurance, healthcare — Health insurance enrollment period (Nov 1–Jan 15). Plan comparisons, enrollment help, deadline reminders.
- **Holiday travel bookings** — types: hospitality, campground — Christmas/New Year availability, winter packages, ski season. Lead time: book now.
- **End-of-year inventory** — types: auto-dealer — Year-end clearance, model-year transitions, best deals of the year.

## December

### Universal hooks

- **Christmas / Hanukkah / holiday season** — types: all — Holiday hours, closures, seasonal greetings, community events. Lead time: ongoing throughout month.
- **New Year's Eve** (Dec 31) — types: all — Year-end celebrations, "last chance" messaging, preview of next year.
- **Year-in-review** — types: all — "Our year in numbers," highlights, milestones, thank-you posts. Strong engagement content.

### Industry hooks

- **Holiday peak** — types: retail, bookshop, florist, grocery, restaurant, salon, brewery — Gift purchases, last-minute shopping, holiday parties, event catering, holiday styles. Ship-by dates for online orders.
- **Year-end tax planning** — types: accounting, insurance, credit-union — Charitable donation deadlines, retirement contribution deadlines, tax-loss harvesting, estimated tax deadline Jan 15 preview.
- **Year-end giving / Giving Tuesday carryover** — types: nonprofit, food-bank, animal-shelter, social-services, house-of-worship, museum — Final push for year-end charitable giving. "Last chance for 2024 tax-deductible donations." Impact reports. Matching gifts.
- **Gift cards push** — types: restaurant, salon, brewery, florist, bookshop, retail, fitness, photography, hospitality — "The gift that always fits." In-store and online availability. Last-minute gift solution.
- **Winter weather prep** — types: trades, hardware, insurance, government — Snow removal, ice management, pipe freezing prevention, winter driving tips.
- **Holiday events** — types: community-theater, museum, brewery, farm, house-of-worship — Holiday performances, exhibit openings, holiday markets, carol services, Hanukkah events, Kwanzaa celebrations.
- **New Year's Eve events** — types: restaurant, brewery, hospitality — Reservation push, special menus, event tickets, overnight packages.
- **Winter break camps** — types: childcare, youth-org — Holiday break programming, registration, activity schedules.
- **Holiday pet safety** — types: pet-services, animal-shelter — Toxic plants, chocolate dangers, fireworks anxiety, boarding for holiday travel.
- **January planning** — types: all — "What's coming in January" teasers, New Year specials preview, resolution-related content prep.
- **Seasonal closures** — types: farm, campground, government — Winter hours, seasonal closure announcements, reopening dates.
