# Q2 — April, May, June

## April

### Universal hooks

- **Easter** (date varies, Mar/Apr) — types: all — Holiday hours, themed events. Restaurants: brunch specials. Retail: gift items. Churches: service schedule. Lead time: 2–3 weeks.
- **Tax Day** (Apr 15) — types: accounting, credit-union, insurance — Filing deadline, extension reminders, "it's not too late" messaging, stress-relief humor.
- **Earth Day** (Apr 22) — types: all — Sustainability practices, environmental commitments, green initiatives. Especially relevant for farm, cleaning, grocery, nonprofit.
- **National Volunteer Week** (3rd week) — types: nonprofit, food-bank, animal-shelter, social-services, youth-org, house-of-worship — Volunteer appreciation, recruitment drives, impact stories.

### Industry hooks

- **Prom season** — types: florist, salon, photography, retail — Corsage and boutonniere pre-orders, hair and makeup bookings, prom portraits, dress shopping. Lead time: 4–6 weeks.
- **National Pet Day** (Apr 11) — types: pet-services, animal-shelter — Adoption specials, pet photo contests, grooming deals, customer pet spotlights.
- **Independent Bookstore Day** (last Saturday) — types: bookshop — Exclusive editions, author events, community celebrations, loyalty promotions.
- **Allergy season** — types: healthcare, cleaning — Allergy management tips, air quality posts, deep cleaning for allergens.
- **Spring sports peak** — types: youth-org, fitness — Season highlights, team photos, tournament announcements, injury prevention tips.
- **Spring mini-sessions** — types: photography — Outdoor portrait sessions, family photos, new location spotlights. Lead time: 3 weeks for booking window.
- **Garden season** — types: hardware, farm — Plant sales, gardening workshops, tool demonstrations, landscaping inspiration.
- **World Book Day** (Apr 23) — types: bookshop, education — Reading celebrations, book donations, literary events.
- **National Beer Day** (Apr 7) — types: brewery — New releases, brewery history, behind-the-scenes, beer trivia.
- **Financial Literacy Month** — types: credit-union, accounting, insurance — Budgeting tips, savings challenges, workshops, youth financial education.
- **Spring moving season begins** — types: storage, cleaning, trades (moving), real-estate — Moving tips, storage specials, cleaning packages, new listing features.

---

## May

### Universal hooks

- **Mother's Day** (2nd Sunday) — types: all — Gift guides, specials, tributes. Florists: #2 revenue day after Valentine's. Restaurants: brunch reservations. Salons: gift certificates. Lead time: 3 weeks.
- **Memorial Day** (last Monday) — types: all — Respectful acknowledgment, community events. Unofficial start of summer. Retail: sales. Hospitality: opening weekend.
- **Graduation season** — types: all — Congratulations posts, graduate specials, party catering/venues, gift ideas.

### Industry hooks

- **International Museum Day** (May 18) — types: museum — Free/discounted admission, special programming, behind-the-scenes tours, collection highlights.
- **Mental Health Awareness Month** — types: healthcare, fitness, social-services — Resources, wellness tips, therapy promotion, community support, stigma reduction.
- **Home improvement season** — types: trades, hardware — Peak season for contractors. Project spotlights, scheduling reminders, outdoor living features.
- **American Craft Beer Week** (mid-May) — types: brewery — Special releases, brewery tours, collaboration brews, industry celebration.
- **Teacher Appreciation Week** (1st full week) — types: education, childcare — Discounts for teachers, thank-you posts, educator spotlights.
- **Provider Appreciation Day** (Fri before Mother's Day) — types: childcare — Celebrate childcare workers, parent thank-you campaigns.
- **Wedding season launches** — types: florist, photography, hospitality, brewery, salon, community-theater — First wave of spring/summer weddings. Share fresh event photos (with permission), booking reminders for fall dates.
- **Farmers market opening** — types: farm, grocery — Season opener announcements, what's available first, market schedules, vendor introductions.
- **Pet Dental Health Month** — types: pet-services — Dental care tips, grooming specials that include dental.
- **Summer camp registration** — types: childcare, youth-org — Last-chance enrollment, program highlights, parent testimonials. Lead time: most camps fill by May.
- **Pool / outdoor season opens** — types: fitness, campground, hospitality — Pool opening announcements, outdoor class schedules, summer programming.
- **National Photography Month** — types: photography — Portfolio highlights, photography tips, client features, mini-session announcements.

---

## June

### Universal hooks

- **Father's Day** (3rd Sunday) — types: all — Gift guides, specials, "for the dad who has everything" angles. Hardware, auto-dealer, brewery especially strong. Lead time: 3 weeks.
- **Pride Month** — types: all — Inclusive messaging, community support, event participation. Authentic involvement, not rainbow-washing.
- **Juneteenth** (Jun 19, federal holiday) — types: all — Community events, historical education, respectful acknowledgment.
- **First day of summer** (Jun 20/21) — types: all — Seasonal transition, outdoor content, summer hours.

### Industry hooks

- **Wedding season peak** — types: florist, photography, hospitality, brewery, salon, community-theater — Busiest month for weddings. Share recent work (with permission), tips for couples getting married this summer.
- **Summer camp begins** — types: childcare, youth-org, campground — First-day photos (with consent), activity previews, parent communication tips.
- **Summer tourism peak begins** — types: hospitality, campground, restaurant, museum, bookshop — Extended hours, visitor guides, local recommendations, event calendars.
- **Farmers market season** — types: farm, grocery, restaurant — Peak produce starts arriving. Weekly "what's fresh" posts, recipe features, market photos.
- **Summer vehicle prep** — types: auto-dealer, repair — Road trip checklists, AC service, tire checks, summer maintenance specials.
- **Outdoor fitness** — types: fitness — Outdoor class schedules, boot camps, running groups, summer challenges.
- **Summer reading programs** — types: bookshop, education — Reading lists, library partnerships, kids' programs, book clubs.
- **Pest season peak** — types: trades (pest control) — Prevention tips, seasonal treatment schedules, common summer pests.
- **Real estate peak season** — types: real-estate — Most active listing period. Market updates, buyer tips, seller prep guides.
- **National Homeownership Month** — types: real-estate, insurance, credit-union, trades — First-time buyer guides, home maintenance checklists, mortgage rate posts.
