# Q1 — January, February, March

## January

### Universal hooks

- **New Year's Day** (Jan 1) — types: all — New year hours, fresh start messaging, "what's new this year" announcements. Good time for a year-ahead preview post.
- **Martin Luther King Jr. Day** (3rd Monday) — types: all — Community service, local events, respectful acknowledgment. Not a sales hook.
- **New Year resolutions** — types: all — How your business helps people with their goals. Fitness, health, financial, organizational, creative.
- **Winter weather** — types: all — Updated hours during storms, safety tips, "we're open" reassurance posts.

### Industry hooks

- **Resolution season** — types: fitness, healthcare, salon, cleaning — New member specials, wellness packages, "new year, new you" without being cliché. Post the first week of January while motivation is high.
- **Dry January** — types: brewery, restaurant — Non-alcoholic options, mocktail menus, NA beer spotlights.
- **Tax prep season begins** — types: accounting, credit-union, insurance — "Get organized" checklists, document gathering reminders, appointment availability. W-2s and 1099s arriving.
- **Winter clearance** — types: retail, bookshop, hardware, grocery — Post-holiday sales, inventory clearance, gift card redemption reminders.
- **Wedding booking season starts** — types: florist, photography, hospitality, brewery, community-theater — Engaged couples from the holidays start booking vendors. Portfolio posts, "book your date" CTAs, venue tours. Lead time: begin now, peak through March.
- **CSA pre-season teasers** — types: farm — What's planned for the growing season, early-bird pricing previews, pickup location scouting. Lead time: sign-ups open in Feb, tease now.
- **Winter maintenance** — types: trades, hardware — Frozen pipe prevention, furnace maintenance, ice dam warnings, generator tips.
- **Year-end financial review** — types: credit-union, insurance — Rate comparisons, account optimization, insurance coverage review. "Start the year with a financial checkup."
- **Winter reading lists** — types: bookshop — Staff picks for the new year, reading challenges, book club launches.
- **Holiday pet intake** — types: animal-shelter — Post-holiday surrender season. Adoption drives, "commitment not a gift" messaging, volunteer needs.
- **Winter camping** — types: campground — For year-round parks: winter site availability, cold-weather camping tips, off-season rates.

## February

### Universal hooks

- **Valentine's Day** (Feb 14) — types: all — Gift guides, specials, promotions, "treat yourself" angles for non-romantic businesses. Lead time: 3 weeks.
- **Presidents' Day** (3rd Monday) — types: all — Sale events, "closed" notices for service businesses.
- **Black History Month** — types: all — Spotlight local Black-owned partners, historical features, community stories. Authentic, not performative.
- **Super Bowl weekend** (1st Sunday, date varies) — types: restaurant, brewery, grocery, hospitality — Watch parties, catering specials, game-day recipes, group bookings.

### Industry hooks

- **Valentine's peak** — types: florist, restaurant, salon, photography, bookshop, jewelry (retail) — Florists: this is the #1 revenue day. Order deadlines, delivery cutoffs, arrangement previews. Restaurants: prix fixe menus, reservation push. Salons: couples packages, "treat yourself." Lead time: 3–4 weeks.
- **Tax season ramp-up** — types: accounting, insurance, credit-union — Document checklists, "what's new in tax law," appointment booking reminders, early filing benefits.
- **CSA sign-up season** — types: farm — Shares go on sale. Early-bird pricing, "what's in a share" explainers, pickup location announcements, member testimonials.
- **Wedding vendor booking push** — types: florist, photography, hospitality, brewery, community-theater — Peak booking window for spring/summer weddings. Showcase recent work, share consultation availability.
- **Spay/Neuter Awareness Month** — types: animal-shelter, pet-services — Low-cost clinic promotions, educational posts, partnership announcements.
- **Heart health / American Heart Month** — types: healthcare, fitness — Heart-healthy tips, fitness challenges, wellness check reminders, CPR class promotion.
- **Random Acts of Kindness Day** (Feb 17) — types: nonprofit, food-bank, social-services, house-of-worship — Volunteer drives, community impact stories, donation pushes.
- **National Pancake Day** (Feb 25, varies) — types: restaurant, farm, grocery — Pancake specials, farm-fresh ingredient features, brunch promotions.
- **Winter pruning / dormant season** — types: farm, hardware — Pruning guides, tool maintenance tips, seed catalog reviews, garden planning.
- **Spring break travel planning** — types: hospitality, campground — Families booking ahead. Availability posts, area activity guides, package deals. Lead time: 4–6 weeks ahead.
- **Pre-spring cleaning push** — types: cleaning, laundry — "Book your spring deep clean early." Lead time: publish now for March/April bookings.
- **Winter storm prep** — types: trades, hardware, insurance, government — Pipe freezing prevention, generator tips, emergency kit checklists, storm damage coverage explainers.
- **Dental health awareness** (Children's Dental Health Month) — types: healthcare — Preventive care tips, schedule reminders for families.
- **National Floral Design Day** (Feb 28) — types: florist — Behind-the-scenes of design process, arrangement tutorials, designer spotlights.

## March

### Universal hooks

- **Daylight Saving Time / spring forward** (2nd Sunday) — types: all — Reminder posts, updated hours if affected, "spring is coming" energy.
- **St. Patrick's Day** (Mar 17) — types: restaurant, brewery, retail, grocery — Themed specials, green everything, Irish food/drink features.
- **Women's History Month** — types: all — Spotlight women on the team, women-owned suppliers, community stories.
- **First day of spring** (Mar 20/21) — types: all — Seasonal transition content, fresh starts, outdoor season begins.

### Industry hooks

- **Spring cleaning** — types: cleaning, laundry, trades, hardware, storage — Deep clean promotions, decluttering tips, garage organization, storage unit specials. Peak booking season for cleaning services.
- **Spring planting begins** — types: farm, hardware, grocery — Seed starting guides, soil prep tips, garden tool spotlights, plant sales. CSA delivery countdown.
- **Spring break** — types: hospitality, campground, restaurant, museum, community-theater — Travel season kicks off. Activity guides, family packages, extended hours, special programming.
- **Lent / Ramadan** (dates vary) — types: house-of-worship, restaurant, grocery — Service schedules, fish fry specials, iftar catering, community meal announcements.
- **Spring wardrobe transition** — types: salon, laundry, retail — New season styles, winter coat cleaning, wardrobe refresh tips.
- **HVAC transition** — types: trades — Furnace-to-AC changeover, spring maintenance appointments, filter replacement reminders.
- **Spring sports registration** — types: youth-org, fitness — League sign-ups, conditioning programs, equipment needs.
- **Home inspection season** — types: real-estate, trades, insurance — Spring market prep, home maintenance checklists, coverage review for new homeowners.
- **Maple sugaring / early harvest** — types: farm — Syrup season content, sugarhouse tours, first-of-the-season products.
- **Spring pet care** — types: pet-services, animal-shelter — Flea/tick season starts, spring grooming, outdoor safety tips.
